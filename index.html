Next steps and priorities

Build the surface, not the duplicates.

The next move is to make Home OS feel simpler than the architecture behind it. These priorities keep the interface useful while the real systems stay canonical.

Immediate next steps

  1. Rename pages to match canonical naming conventions.
  2. Assign owners for each overlapping domain.
  3. Convert placeholders into clear pointers.
  4. Move only the truly misplaced pages instead of reshuffling everything.
  5. Mark legacy duplicates once link replacement is complete.

Top feature priorities

  • Guided Start with a three-button front door: Today, Add Something, Find Something.
  • One-tap Family Inbox capture with pre-labeled task, question, reminder, and idea routes.
  • Hub cards dashboard with icon, description, and click-through clarity.
  • Inbox triage board using New, Needs Decision, Routed, and Done.
  • Canonical badges that visibly mark pointer pages vs. systems of record.

Feature themes

  • Navigation and wayfinding through guided start, hub cards, and route clarity.
  • Capture and triage through Family Inbox, triage board, and review queues.
  • Roles and guardrails through family mode surfaces, safe edit zones, and admin separation.
  • Family operations through Today views, meals entry, and house quick links.
  • Maintainability through link checks, canonical badges, and hub registry hygiene.
